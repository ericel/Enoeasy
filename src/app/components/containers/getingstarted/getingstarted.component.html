Sharing a File

Our service provides you with the opportunity to share in most file formats, word documents, pdf, audio , videos and more.

As an authenticated member , you can share at the cost of 5 Credit points per file. At this point we have two sharing options (free file and premium file).

Free File:

If you share a file as free, it will be available to service users for free. Users will be able to download such a file with by no cost. This will be an ideal option if you are one of those who just wants his art available to everyone else for free.

Premium File:

Premium files go with a price tag. You are allowed to set the price for your file. Let say a $2 (5 credit points) for a music audio file).

With this option, service users are expected to pay a token to be able to either read, listen or download such a file.

When a file is listed as premium, we will seek to create a preview of the file which will be available to service users.

For every file sold, we take a compensation for service. You have nothing to loose

You can always contact the help desk for more support or look through the forums for help.
